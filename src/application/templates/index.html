Welcome to My 3 Pages - Morning Pages on the Web

Unblock your creativity by typing 3 pages every day on any topic you want. Inspired by Julia Cameron's Morning Pages in her book, "The Artist's Way", the goal is simple.

Write 3 pages on anything you want. It doesn't need to make sense or have correct spelling. Just do a brain dump of whatever is on your mind

To help motivate you there is a word counter and a progress bar to show you how you're tracking.

All you need is a Google account to login. So give it a go and happy creative unblocking!
