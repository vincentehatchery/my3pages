Ever had writer's block? Did you have something you wanted to say, but couldn't express it? Well this is a tool to help you. Well similarly to an exercise, the first page is usually a warm up. It gets rid of all random thoughts and just performs a tuning of what you can actually write. If you are pushed to write 3 pages, try the inspiration button on the right to help with ideas. Happy artist unblocking!
